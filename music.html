Boney M - Music Collection

Boney M. was a Euro-Caribbean vocal group created by German record producer Frank Farian in 1975. They became one of the most popular disco acts in the 1970s and early '80s, known for hits like "Rasputin", "Rivers of Babylon", and "Daddy Cool". Their vibrant costumes and catchy beats helped them achieve massive commercial success, selling over 150 million records worldwide. Boney M. was also notable for their visually dynamic performances and a unique mix of disco, pop, and reggae influences which left a lasting impact on the international music scene.
